Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

StudyLeem is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

Information We DO Collect:

  • Anonymous Usage Data: We may collect anonymous statistics about page views and downloads to improve our service
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device information, and IP address for security and performance purposes

Information We DO NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account information (we don't require user accounts)
  • Payment information (all materials are free)
  • Location data beyond country-level for analytics

2. How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely to:

  • Maintain and improve website functionality
  • Understand which materials are most helpful to students
  • Protect against abuse and security threats
  • Optimize website performance

3. Cookies

StudyLeem may use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences (such as selected class level)
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Improve user experience

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect some website functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

StudyLeem uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For database and hosting services
  • Google Drive: For PDF file hosting and delivery
  • Google Fonts: For website typography

These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

StudyLeem is designed for students in classes 9-12 (typically ages 14-18). We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone. Parents and guardians should supervise their children's internet usage.

7. External Links

Our website contains links to external sites (such as Google Drive). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies before using them.

8. Your Rights

As we don't collect personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. However, you have the right to:

  • Request information about our data practices
