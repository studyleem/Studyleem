Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Information We Collect

StudyLeem collects minimal data to provide our free service:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time on site (via Google Analytics)
  • Contact Messages: Name, email, and message when you use our contact form
  • Cookies: Google Analytics and Google AdSense use cookies for analytics and advertising

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to contact form submissions
  • To improve our study materials and site experience
  • To serve relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • To analyze site traffic via Google Analytics

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic analysis
  • Google AdSense — advertising
  • Firebase (Google) — database for study materials and contact messages
  • Google Drive — PDF file hosting and viewing
  • Vercel — website hosting and analytics

Data Security

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Contact form submissions are stored securely in Firebase and only accessible to site administrators. We do not require user accounts or store passwords.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies from Google Analytics and Google AdSense. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

Children's Privacy

Our site is designed for students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond what is necessary to respond to a contact form message.

Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us or email support@studyleem.com.
