About StudyLeem

Helping Federal Board Pakistan students access free quality study materials

Who We Are

StudyLeem is a free online platform dedicated to Federal Board (FBISE) students in Pakistan. We provide high-quality, chapter-wise notes, textbooks and math exercise solutions for Class 9, 10, 11 and 12 — completely free of charge.

Our mission is simple: every student in Pakistan deserves access to good study materials, regardless of their financial situation. Quality education should not have a price tag.

What We Offer

We provide the following free study materials for FBISE Federal Board students:

  • Chapter-wise Notes — Organized by class, subject and chapter number for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, English, Urdu and more.
  • Math Exercise Solutions — Individual exercise PDFs (e.g. Exercise 1.1, 1.2, 2.3) so you can jump directly to the exercise you need.
  • FBISE Textbooks — Official Federal Board textbooks in PDF format for all major subjects.
  • PDF Viewer — View all materials directly in your browser without needing to download anything.

Classes We Cover

  • Class 9 (SSC Part 1) — FBISE notes and books
  • Class 10 (SSC Part 2) — FBISE notes and books
  • Class 11 (HSSC Part 1) — FBISE notes and books
  • Class 12 (HSSC Part 2) — FBISE notes and books

Our Commitment

We are committed to keeping StudyLeem free, ad-supported, and growing. We regularly add new chapters, exercises and subjects based on student requests. If you don't find what you need, contact us and we'll prioritize adding it.

Disclaimer

All study materials on StudyLeem are shared for educational purposes only. We do not claim ownership of official FBISE textbook content. If you believe any content violates copyright, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.
